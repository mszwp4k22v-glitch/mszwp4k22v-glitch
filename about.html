Baked with Heart

Crumbs & Co. is a small-batch baking blog born in a home kitchen and fueled by curiosity, flour clouds, and too many sticks of butter.

Our Story

It Started With a Sourdough Disaster

In the spring of 2020, like thousands of others, we became obsessed with sourdough. Our first loaf was a dense, gummy, sad little brick — but we were hooked.

What followed was two years of reading, experimenting, failing, and finally succeeding. We discovered that baking is fundamentally about understanding: the science of gluten, the biology of fermentation, the chemistry of caramelization.

Crumbs & Co. exists to share everything we’ve learned, in plain language, for home bakers who want to truly understand their craft — not just follow instructions blindly.

“Baking is the only culinary art that demands equal parts precision and patience — and rewards you with something that fills a house with warmth.”

— Founder, Crumbs & Co.
